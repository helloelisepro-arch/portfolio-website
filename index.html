About

A decade of work across three languages, four companies, and a lot of paper.

I've spent that decade making complex things clear — IP law, data privacy, brand protection, creative education — for audiences who need to understand them quickly and act on them confidently.

I'm trilingual (English, French, Spanish), I've worked across Barcelona, Mendoza, and Marseille, and I've led content for campaigns that ran in eight languages across six channels simultaneously.

Three years ago I was an early casualty of AI displacement in content. It was a turning point. I spent time understanding what that shift meant before re-entering — I wanted to come back on the strategy side, not just survive the change. Now I work at the intersection of editorial instinct, strategic thinking, and AI-augmented production.

I know what good looks like. And I know how to build the systems that produce it consistently.
